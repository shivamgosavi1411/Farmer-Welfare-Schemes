Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Brief Overview: ₹6000/year DBT in 3 installments

Objective: Provide income support to small and marginal farmers

Eligibility: Small/marginal farmers, Aadhaar-seeded bank accounts

Benefits: ₹6000/year in 3 equal installments

Application Process: Online registration through the official portal

Apply Now / Official Site

PM-KISAN Guide (Seekho)

Video: How to apply

Required Documents/Certificates:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land ownership documents
  • Bank account details
  • Income certificate (if required)

Q: Who is eligible for PM-KISAN?

A: Small and marginal farmers with Aadhaar-seeded bank accounts.

Q: How often are payments made?

A: Payments are made in 3 installments per year.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Brief Overview: Low-premium crop insurance

Objective: Provide financial support to farmers in case of crop failure

Eligibility: All farmers (voluntary), for notified crops

Benefits: Low-premium crop insurance coverage

Application Process: Online registration through the official portal

Apply Now / Official Site

PMFBY explained (AgriApp)

Application tutorial

Required Documents/Certificates:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land ownership documents
  • Crop insurance details
  • Bank account details

Q: Is PMFBY mandatory?

A: No, it's voluntary for all farmers.

Q: What crops are covered?

A: All notified crops in the district.

PM-KUSUM Scheme

Brief Overview: Solar pump subsidy (up to 90%)

Objective: Promote solar energy in agriculture

Eligibility: Farmers, cooperatives, Panchayats

Benefits: Up to 90% subsidy for solar pumps

Application Process: Online registration through the official portal

Apply Now / Official Portal

Govt PDF Guide

YouTube Demo

Required Documents/Certificates:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land ownership documents
  • Electricity bill (if applicable)
  • Bank account details

Q: What is the subsidy amount for solar pumps?

A: Up to 90% of the cost.

Q: Who can apply for PM-KUSUM?

A: Farmers, cooperatives, and Panchayats.

Formation & Promotion of Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)

Brief Overview: Collective support, training, credit access

Objective: Strengthen farmer collectives for better market access

Eligibility: Farmer groups, women, SHGs

Benefits: Training, credit facilities, market linkages

Application Process: Contact local agricultural department or SFAC

Official Info

FPO Handbook

Online Training

Required Documents/Certificates:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Group registration certificate
  • Bank account details
  • Training completion certificates (if applicable)

Q: What is an FPO?

A: Farmer Producer Organization that helps farmers collectively market their produce.

Q: How can I form an FPO?

A: Contact your local agricultural department or SFAC for guidance.

Krishi Samruddhi Yojana (Maharashtra)

Brief Overview: Micro-irrigation, low-cost mechanization, organic farming

Objective: Promote sustainable agriculture practices in Maharashtra

Eligibility: Maharashtra farmers, especially SC/ST, women

Benefits: Subsidies for micro-irrigation and mechanization

Application Process: Contact local agricultural department

Official News

State Department Site

Marathi News Article

Required Documents/Certificates:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land ownership documents
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Bank account details

Q: What benefits does this scheme offer?

A: Subsidies for micro-irrigation, low-cost mechanization, and organic farming.

Q: Who can apply?

A: Maharashtra farmers, especially SC/ST and women.
